The V Foundation

Our Story

The V Foundation for Cancer reasearch was founed by ESPN and basketball coach Jimmy Valvano to defeat cancer. Since its formation in 1993, the V Foundation has awarded over $310 million in cancer research grants nationwide and has grown to become one of the premier supporters of cutting-edge cancer research. The Delta Chi Fraternity joined this foundation with the same mindset and has dedicated itself to the V Foundation for Cancer Research. Jimmy Valvano's hard-fought dream of a better tomorrow embodies the spirt of Delta Chi. His famous words of strength, "Don't Give Up...Don't Ever Give Up!" resonate with every brother.
