Our Mission Statement

As Delta Chi our mission is to encourage and promote intellectual curiosity through academic achievement and to develop the personal and social skills of students by providing leadership opportunities through self-governance. As well as, promote service through the University's co-curricular programs and through community involvement.

We are looking for people who are willing to make an impact on the world around us and to become leaders of our generation and future generations to come.
